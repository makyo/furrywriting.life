Welcome

Furry Writing Life is a site for projects undertaken by members of the Furry Writers' Guild and other furry writers out there on the internet.

Projects

When new projects are added, they'll be shown here!

Anthologies @ FWLife

Anthologies collects anthologies from within the furry writing sphere and offers them a place to host a custom page and call details.

Check out Anthologies @ FWLife!

Furry Writing Knowledge Base

The FWLife Knowledge Base stores information about the users and what their expertise is. It is intended as a resource for writers to let them know who to ask about what.

Check out Furry Writing Knowledge Base!

Song Stories

Song Stories is a project for short stories, vignettes, and flash fiction inspired by, about, or including a song.

Check out Song Stories!
